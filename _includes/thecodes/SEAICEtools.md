Julia Package to work with remote sensing data of Sea Ice.
At this point only the following data providers are supported:
Institute of Environmental Physics (IEP) at the University of Bremen:
AMSRE -AMSR2: SIC Arctic-3.125km @ 5 km
Merged AMSR2-MODIS: SIC @ 1 km
SENTINEL-1A by ESA provided: lead fraction (LF) and sea ice divergence (DIV) @ 0.7 km
OSI SAF EUMETSAT: SSMIS, AMSR2 SIC @ 10 km
